Theme

We invite short research papers and full papers on analytics, machine learning (ML), and artificial intelligence (AI) from researchers, industry professionals, and students to showcase their novel and innovative work.

Submission must be related to the theme of the conference

Smart Systems, Smarter Decisions: Data Insights for a Connected and Sustainable Future

The focus should be aligned with, but not limited to:

Analytics methods, tools, and techniques for Smart Systems, Smarter Decisions:

  • Data collection and preprocessing for analytics and AI/ML
  • Predictive and prescriptive analytics
  • Machine learning
  • Deep learning
  • Social media analysis
  • Text, image and video analytics

Application areas:

  • Application of analytics for smart system and smart decisions
